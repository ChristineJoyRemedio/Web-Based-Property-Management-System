Pueblo El Grande Subdivision

Pueblo El Grande is an exclusive residential enclave that boasts of 158 units in six architectural designs inspired by the sun-drenched Spanish-Mediterranean villas of Southern Europe. Among its many features, we take particular pride in our state-of-the-art fire and intrusion alarm system. Each home is linked to Pueblo El Grande’s centralized monitoring system which allows the enclave’s security personnel to monitor your house from intrusion and accidental fire 24 hours a day. This system, unique to Pueblo El Grande, is directly connected to the Consolacion Fire and Police Stations.
